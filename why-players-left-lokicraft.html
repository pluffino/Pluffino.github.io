Why Many Players Have Moved On From Lokicraft

Published by Pluffino Gaming Hub

1. Too Many Bugs And Glitches

In my experience, Lokicraft has far more bugs and glitches than similar games such as Craftsman and Crafting & Building.

2. Too Many Advertisements

Even when playing offline, advertisements can appear regularly and interrupt gameplay.

3. Hard To Find The Original Version

Many Android players use the Google Play Store, but nowadays it can be difficult to find the original Lokicraft there.
