How To Get Paid Add-ons For Free In Crafting & Building

Many players think they need to spend real money to get the best add-ons in Crafting & Building. Fortunately, there is a free method available in the game.

Step 1

Make sure you have a good internet connection.

Step 2

Launch Crafting & Building.

Step 3

Tap the Play button.

Step 4

Open the Store section.

Step 5

Browse through the available add-ons. Some add-ons are completely free, while others require coins.

Step 6

To get coins for free, use the ad reward system. Watch advertisements to earn coins.

Step 7

Each ad rewards you with 20 coins.

Step 8

Watch up to 7 ads each day and save your coins.

Step 9

Once you have enough coins, purchase any add-on you want from the store.

Done!

By using the free coin system, you can unlock premium add-ons without spending real money.
