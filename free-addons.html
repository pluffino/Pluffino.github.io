How To Get Paid Add-ons For Free In Crafting & Building

Published by Pluffino Gaming Hub

Many players think they need to spend real money to unlock the best add-ons in Crafting & Building. However, the game includes a coin system that allows players to earn coins for free.

Step 1: Get A Stable Internet Connection

Before starting, make sure your internet connection is working properly. The reward system requires an online connection.

Step 2: Open Crafting & Building

Launch the game and wait for the main menu to load completely.

Step 3: Open The Store

Tap the Play button and then open the Store section. Here you will find many add-ons, maps, skins, and other content.

Step 4: Check The Available Add-ons

Some add-ons are completely free, while others require coins to unlock. Usually, the more advanced add-ons cost more coins.

Step 5: Earn Coins By Watching Ads

If your internet connection is working, the game will allow you to watch advertisements in exchange for coins.

Each advertisement rewards you with approximately 20 coins.

Step 6: Save Your Coins

Instead of spending coins immediately, save them for the add-ons you really want.

If you watch around 7 ads every day, you can slowly build up enough coins to purchase many premium add-ons without spending real money.

Why This Method Is Useful

Many players spend money to buy coins, but patient players can earn the same rewards simply by using the ad system regularly.

This makes Crafting & Building one of the few Minecraft-style games where premium content can be unlocked completely free.

Final Thoughts

If you have a good internet connection and a little patience, you can unlock many paid add-ons without spending any real money.
