How To Get Free Emotes In Craftsman

Published by Pluffino Gaming Hub

Getting free emotes in Craftsman is very easy because most emotes are already available in the game.

Step 1

Turn on your internet connection.

Step 2

Launch Craftsman.

Step 3

Create a new world or join any existing world.

Step 4

Once you are in the game, tap the Emotes button at the top of the screen.

Step 5

Tap the Change button.

Step 6

Scroll through the available emotes.

Step 7

Choose the emote you want and equip it.

Done!

You now have a free emote equipped and ready to use in Craftsman.
