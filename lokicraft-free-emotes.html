How To Get Free Emotes In Lokicraft

Published by Pluffino Gaming Hub

Many Lokicraft players don't know that free emotes are already available in the game. If you have an internet connection, you can equip them in just a few steps.

Step 1: Turn On Your Internet Connection

Make sure your device is connected to the internet before opening the game.

Step 2: Launch Lokicraft

Open Lokicraft and wait for the game to load.

Step 3: Enter A World

Create a new world or join an existing one.

Step 4: Open The Emotes Menu

Once you are in-game, look at the top of the screen and tap the Emotes button.

Step 5: Tap Change

Press the Change button to view the available emotes.

Step 6: Browse The Emotes

Scroll through the list and check out all the emotes that Lokicraft currently offers.

Step 7: Equip An Emote

Choose your favorite emote and equip it to your character.

Done!

You can now use your selected emote while playing Lokicraft.

Most of these emotes are available completely free, making it easy to customize your gameplay experience.

Final Thoughts

With a working internet connection, getting free emotes in Lokicraft only takes a minute and gives your character more personality.
