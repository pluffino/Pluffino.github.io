How To Get Free Emotes In Lokicraft

Getting free emotes in Lokicraft is very easy because most emotes are already available in the game.

Step 1

Turn on your internet connection.

Step 2

Launch Lokicraft.

Step 3

Create a new world or join any existing world.

Step 4

Once you are in the game, look at the top of the screen and tap the Emotes button.

Step 5

Tap the Change button.

Step 6

Scroll through the list of available emotes.

Step 7

Choose the emote you want and equip it.

Done!

You now have a free emote equipped. Lokicraft includes many emotes that players can use without spending money.
