How To Get Free Emotes In Crafting & Building

Published by Pluffino Gaming Hub

Many players don't realize that most emotes in Crafting & Building can be unlocked and equipped completely free. You only need an internet connection and a few taps.

Step 1: Turn On Your Internet Connection

Make sure your device is connected to the internet before launching the game.

Step 2: Open Crafting & Building

Launch the game and wait for it to load completely.

Step 3: Enter A World

Create a new world or join any world you already have.

Step 4: Open The Emotes Menu

Once you are inside the game, look at the top of the screen and tap the Emotes button.

Step 5: Tap Change

After opening the emotes menu, tap the Change button.

Step 6: Browse The Available Emotes

Scroll through the list and you will find all the emotes that Crafting & Building currently offers.

Step 7: Equip Your Favorite Emote

Choose the emote you like and equip it to your character.

Done!

You can now use your selected emote while playing the game.

One of the best things about Crafting & Building is that many of its emotes are available without spending any money.

Final Thoughts

If your internet connection is working properly, getting free emotes takes less than a minute and gives you more ways to express yourself in-game.
