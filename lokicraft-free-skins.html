How To Get Free Skins In lokicraft

Getting free skins in lokicraft is very easy. Most skins in the game are available for free.

Step 1

Turn on your internet connection.

Step 2

Launch lokicraft.

Step 3

On the main menu, tap the Change Skin button.

Step 4

Wait a few seconds for the skins to load.

Step 5

If your internet connection is working properly, you will see many free skins and presets.

Step 6

Choose any skin you like and equip it.

Done!

You now have a new skin equipped. Most of the skins available in lokicraft can be used completely free of charge.
